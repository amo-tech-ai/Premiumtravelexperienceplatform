# Figma Phase 1 (Supplement): CTA & Footer

**Goal:** Innovative closing sections for the Home Page to drive conversion and provide site-wide navigation.
**Dependency:** Appends to Prompt 1.1 (Home Screen).

---

## Prompt 1.3: Pre-Footer CTA Section

**Purpose:**
Create a high-impact transition CTA placed directly above the footer that moves users from browsing into the AI Concierge system. This CTA should feel like: "Before you leave — let the concierge take over."

**Placement Rules:**
*   Full-width section.
*   Sits immediately above the footer on all marketing and discovery pages.
*   Appears once per page (no repetition).

**Instructions:**
Design a **Pre-Footer CTA Section** that introduces and links to the AI Concierge system.

### 1. Visual Style & Layout
*   **Layout:**
    *   Full-width horizontal section.
    *   Max content width centered (matching page grid).
    *   Generous vertical padding (luxury spacing).
*   **Background:**
    *   Soft off-white or very light emerald tint.
    *   Optional subtle gradient or noise texture.
    *   Must visually separate content from the footer.
*   **Tone:** Calm. Confident. Concierge-level. Never salesy.

### 2. Content Structure (Top → Bottom)
*   **Eyebrow Text:** Small, "Not sure what to choose?"
*   **Headline:** Large, Serif. "Let the Concierge Plan It For You".
*   **Supporting Text:** "Events, restaurants, stays, and experiences — intelligently connected in one place."
*   **Primary CTA Button:**
    *   **Label:** "Ask the Concierge"
    *   **Style:** Rounded-xl or pill.
    *   **Background:** Deep Emerald (`#064E3B`).
    *   **Text:** White.
    *   **Icon:** Sparkle / AI star on the left.
    *   **Hover:** Soft gold glow + slight lift.
*   **Micro-hint (Below Button):** "Takes less than a minute"

### 3. Responsive Behavior
*   **Mobile:**
    *   Content stacked vertically.
    *   Button full-width.
    *   Reduced headline size.
    *   Comfortable thumb reach.
*   **Desktop:**
    *   Centered column layout.
    *   Headline visually dominant.
    *   CTA clearly isolated.

### 4. Variants
*   **Standard:** As described above.
*   **Contextual (Optional):**
    *   *Events Page:* Headline → "Not sure what to do tonight?"
    *   *Real Estate Page:* Headline → "Want help finding the right investment?"

## Prompt 1.4: Global Footer

**Context:**
The persistent footer at the bottom of every page. It needs to convey trust and provide secondary navigation.

**Instructions:**
Design the **Global Footer**.

### 1. Layout Structure
*   **Desktop:** 4-Column Grid.
    *   **Col 1:** Brand (Logo + Tagline).
    *   **Col 2:** Discover (Links to Modes: Eat, Play, Stay, Visit).
    *   **Col 3:** Company (About, Contact, Careers).
    *   **Col 4:** Social & Legal (Instagram/Twitter icons + Copyright).
*   **Mobile:** Stacked vertical layout with center alignment.

### 2. Styling
*   **Background:** Almost Black (`#111827`) or very dark Emerald.
*   **Text:** White (Headings) and Light Grey (Links).
*   **Typography:**
    *   *Headings:* Uppercase, Tracking Wide, Gold color.
    *   *Links:* `Inter`, clean, underline on hover.

### 3. Elements
*   **Brand:** "Medellín AI" (Logo).
*   **Newsletter (Optional):** "Subscribe for weekly curated picks." Simple input + arrow button.
*   **Copyright:** "© 2024 Medellín AI Concierge. All rights reserved."
